About Me

I'm a designer living in Palo Alto, California.

I began my journey in design by building a few side projects in HTML/CSS/Javascript in my spare time. Coupled with my almost-lifelong interests in technology and creativity, working on these projects evolved into a passion for creating useful, enjoyable, and accessible digital experiences.

Soon after, I started my design career at Carta, where I designed and shipped multiple features for public and private enterprise companies. I'm passionate about design and I strive to create high-quality work by effectively addressing user and business needs, and developing a thorough understanding of the problems I'm solving.

I'm a curious person 🧐 and I seek to understand how people and things work.
